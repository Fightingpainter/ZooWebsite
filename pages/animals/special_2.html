The Ancient Titan

The Ancient Titan is a creature of immense size and age. In our aquarium, it requires a habitat of 9,826,675,000,000 square meters and a significant amount of food. We provide special care to meet its needs, understanding the importance of preserving this remarkable creature.

In the wild, the Ancient Titan is the largest animal on the planet, with a body averaging about 10,000,000,000m long. There are only eight known to exist in the world. These beings are almost as old as the planet itself, and it's said that they can live indefinitely. Early humans saw them as gods due to their immense size and longevity. Today, they require special care from aquariums to get the amount of food they need, as their natural food sources have become scarce.
